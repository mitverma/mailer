Swedish Nomad

Here’s a Swedish guy, Alex, who dreamt of travelling the world since childhood and finally realised it when he took a course in bartending in the Canary Islands. He loved the independence while there and decided to go for an unconventional lifestyle rather than a 9-5 job. He has travelled to 43 countries since along with his girlfriend and is still counting. Alex narrates his travels on his blog, Swedish Nomad, and seeks to inspire everyone to travel more and unravel this beautiful world we live in.
