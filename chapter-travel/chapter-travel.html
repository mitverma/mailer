Chapter Travel

The young couple Lissane and Jeffrey love to travel, discover the world and its hidden gems and share their experiences with everyone. Right from giving priceless tips on travelling through Asia, to tips for every kind of traveller, be it solo, adventure, budget or any other, they narrate their experiences hoping to help those with wanderlust to travel freely and safely on their blog, Chapter Travel. They believe that if you can travel, you are certainly privileged; but yes, if you hold a passport, it makes it easier.
