New On TraveLibro

Nomads, wanderers, explorers, wayfarers, travellers...no matter what we name them, their tribe is increasing and changing and even determining the way we travel. Here’s a list of popular travel professionals who recently signed up on the TraveLibro platform and narrate their incredible and interesting travel tales here.

Girl vs Globe

A girl with a strong determination decides at the age of 10 to go after what she wants, which is to move to London one day and even achieves it by the time she is 17. Sabina, the go-getter from Czech Republic who currently lives in Scotland, travels the world today and documents and shares her experiences for others. But that’s not all...she calls herself a responsible traveller and offers travel tips on everything from eco-friendly travel to sustainable fashion, vegan recipes, and much more. This itch for wanderlust has taken Sabina across the length and breadth of Europe taking a piece with her from Ireland, Germany, Croatia, and even Morocco as well as many others. She has travelled to Slovenia too...you can check out her travel story on TraveLibro and immerse yourself in her interesting tales.

How Far From home

They call themselves creative thinkers who decided to cut away from the predictable, ordinary and uninspiring. But they had it all...great jobs, a lovely home, shiny cars, a great family and fabulous friends. Yet, complacency over time prompted Chanel and Stevo to give it all up for a life full of unpredictability sprinkled with generous doses of excitement and thrill of the unknown and unfamiliar. So, from advertising and branding they moved away from their comfort zone and decided to explore the world. They’ve in fact, travelled to over 35 countries and 113 cities and is all fired up to take on more. These nomads have been through Berlin, Madrid, Thailand, London, and explored each destination in a unique way. Browse through their beautiful journeys about their cool experiences at Solta Island, Croatia and Salzburgerland, Austria on TraveLibro, making you want to take a trip there too.

No Destinations

It started with a casual ‘let’s take a sabbatical’ thought over regular cocktails voiced by Chris to which Danika merely rolled her eyes. But, a few cocktails later, they decided to take six months off and travel the globe. Acting upon the plan, they have travelled across the world since 2014 quitting their high-profile jobs to soak in diverse cultures and savour a variety of cuisines and drinks. Both were seeking a sense of adventure and a better work/life balance and as they candidly put, they could give up everything to do this. So they did...and have travelled to more than 90 destinations since 2014 and gone trekking through the rainforests of Malaysia and on a, Icelandic glacier; explored the deserts of UAE and even swum with turtles in the Maldives. This nomadic couple has documented their explorations through Sweden, Denmark, and the Czech Republic on our platform as well.

Chapter Travel

The young couple Lissane and Jeffrey love to travel and discover the world and its hidden gems at their own pace and sharing their experiences with everyone. Right from giving priceless tips on travelling through Asia, to tips for every kind of traveller, be it solo, adventure, budget or any other, they narrate their experiences hoping to help those with wanderlust to travel freely and safely. They believe that if you can travel, you are certainly privileged for you have the means to and are in circumstances that allow you to do so. But yes, if you hold a passport, it makes it easier to travel across countries and take in amazing experiences. These young wayfarers have explored Australia, Cambodia, India, Vietnam, Thailand in the continent of Asia, England Germany, Scotland, Hungary, The Netherlands, in Europe and continuing their journeys across the globe. Check out their trips and experiences through London, Scotland, Japan, Korea, and Nepal on TraveLibro.

Globetrotting Blonde

After completing her graduation in Political Science and Television & Radio Production, she took up a job as a commercial producer at a local news station almost immediately. But, she gave it all up for a backpacking tour through Europe and has not stopped travelling and exploring since. Meet Fiorella Bertolla, who discovered that there were many who wished to travel but were unsure about how to. And so, she attempts to make affordable travel a reality hoping to serve as an inspiration to others to follow their dreams, through her tips and stories. This young and spunky globetrotter has been to the Caribbean, North America, Mexico, South America, several countries in Asia, and is unstoppable. Browse through her journeys of Belize and Uruguay on TraveLibro to know more about her interesting travel tales.

Dopes on the Road

She calls herself a ‘queer millenial’, who along with her partner has a passion for wanderlust and loves narrating their tales to the world. Meg, the other half of Meg and Lindsay, hit a saturation point in her efforts for the queer community and moved to South Korea to teach ESL, subsequently starting a blog to record her teachings, which quickly turned into a way to communicate with family and friends and eventually, into a narrative of their travels and catching on with the community. Since then, it was no looking back and now Meg endeavours to inspire others to have adventures in daily life, just like them as they travel through Peru, Europe, South and North America, Asia, etc. The LGBT community though, faces difficulties and safety issues in many countries and so, the duo attempt to lead by example and inspire the community to explore the world fearlessly. Read through their travel story to Peru on TraveLibro and get inspired to travel more.

Mumpack Travel

Gutsy is perhaps a small adjective to describe this feisty lady...for veering from the norm she decided to go after what she desired and embarked on a travel spree across Asia with her five-year-old tot. New-age mom Evie, along with her daughter Emmie, has been trailblazing through Bali, Borneo, Cambodia, China, Fiji, Taipei, Taiwan, Maldives, Philippines, Sri Lanka, Cologne, a road trip in Uluru, Australia, and many other exotic destinations. Evie wanted to offer her little girl an unusual learning experience of life overall. She hopes to give her daughter amazing cultural experiences and understanding, and tolerating differences that come with travel. Evie had saved up for a renovation project at home but instead used up her savings to fund her year-long wanderlust to Asia. She loved creating a wonderful tale to Indonesia on TraveLibro and in turn attempts to inspire other parents to give their children the same experiences that come through discovering the world. You can even follow their interesting tale to Disneyland and LA on our platform.
