The Tiny Taster

Books, movies, travel and food are her passions...more so food and travel. In fact, food is an obsession and she loves to explore the world tasting its diverse cuisines. The pint-sized Roxanne Bamboat, hopes to give insight about the destinations she travels to along with the food there, on her blog—The Tiny Taster. Traverse through her incredible and interesting journeys to the corners of the globe and immerse yourself in a delightfully delicious experience. And, Roxanne puts it aptly...she loves to take the world by storm—one plate at a time.
